Tanks is a 2D side view strategy game where each player will have to use their strategy, skill and wit to eliminate the other before they eliminate you. What happens if I simply keep typing until I am off screen? Does it wrap around nicely or just disappear? I know what my bets are on!

How To Play
Player 1
A/D - Move
W/S - Aim

Player 2
Left Arrow/Right Arrow - Move
Up Arrow/Down Arrow - Aim

Both Players use Space bar to fire
